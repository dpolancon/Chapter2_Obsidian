# 02A_Research_Question

## Governing question

How can capacity utilization be reconstructed as a socially mediated outcome of capitalist reproduction, rather than treated as a given macroeconomic ratio?

## Operational question

How does this reconstructed object behave across structurally unequal positions—specifically, in a canonical central case and in Chile as a peripheral formation within a hierarchically organized world of states?
