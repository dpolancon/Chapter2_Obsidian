# Normal reproduction closure

## Core claim

The transformation elasticity is defined relative to a benchmark of smooth reproduction.

---

## Definition

$$
g_{K,t}^N = \chi_t^N r_t^N - \delta
$$

with:

- $r_t^N$ → normal profit rate  
- $\chi_t^N$ → normal reinvestment ratio  
- $\delta$ → depreciation  

---

## Conditions

- $\mu_t = 1$
- capacity is fully utilized  
- accumulation and capacity grow proportionally  

---

## Role in the model

The normal accumulation path provides the reference against which transformation is evaluated.

---

## Locked formulation

All transformation relations are defined relative to the normal reproduction benchmark, ensuring a consistent separation between structural dynamics and observed deviations.
