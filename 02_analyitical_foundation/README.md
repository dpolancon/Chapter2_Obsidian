## Series Conventions in 02_analytical_foundation/
- **A-series (A01–A04)**: Analytical mechanism definitions. Derivations, object definitions, structural operations.
- **H-series (H00–H01)**: Conceptual hinges. Bridge ontological framing to analytical mechanisms.
- **N-series (N00 only)**: Reference/support notes. Intellectual genealogy, equation–source mapping, traceability. *Does not derive or define.*
- **R-series (R05–R07)**: Rules, diagnostic protocols, ontological guardrails.
