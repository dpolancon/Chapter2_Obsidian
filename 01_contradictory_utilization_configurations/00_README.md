## Main thesis

The firm-level normal degree of capacity utilization presupposes a unified firm, a stable capacity denominator, and a coherent utilization benchmark. This bundle shows that all three are fractured across labor process, managerial hierarchy, governance, operations, and measurement conventions.

A contradictory utilization configuration is a firm-level organizational settlement among labor discipline, worker cognition, workflow, buffers, managerial discretion, learning, governance pressure, and capacity conventions.

[[Utilization regimes]] name the higher-order historical and institutional layer that organizes, stabilizes, and distributes configurations across firms, sectors, and periods.
