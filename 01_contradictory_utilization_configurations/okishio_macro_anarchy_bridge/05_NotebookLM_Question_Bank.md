# NotebookLM Question Bank

Use these prompts when interrogating Okishio source material.

## Macro mechanism

1. How does Okishio explain the relation between decentralized accumulation and disproportionality?
2. Where does idle capacity enter the mechanism?
3. Does idle capacity appear as technical slack, market failure, crisis symptom, or normal capitalist condition?
4. How does Okishio distinguish equilibrium accumulation from crisis-prone accumulation?

## Firm abstraction

5. Does Okishio treat the firm as internally unified?
6. What assumptions are made about firm decision-making?
7. Are labor-process contradictions visible, absent, or abstracted away?
8. Does the framework require a coherent firm-level utilization target?

## Bridge to main bundle

9. Where can [[unified firm abstraction]] be identified?
10. How does the macro-anarchy mechanism justify the need for [[contradictory utilization configurations]]?
11. How can Okishio be used without collapsing the firm back into a unified profit-maximizer?
