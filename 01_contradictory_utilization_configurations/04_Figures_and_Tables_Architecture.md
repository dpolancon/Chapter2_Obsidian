# Visual and table architecture

## Figure 1. The hidden assumption of utilization theory

Unified firm → coherent capacity denominator → normal utilization benchmark

Breakdown:

- Unified firm → fractured across labor process, managerial tiers, and governance conflict.
- Coherent capacity denominator → multiple conventions: engineering, accounting, practical, survey, macro-statistical.
- Normal utilization benchmark → splits into planned, realized, reported, and theoretical-normal objects.

Caption:

The normal degree presupposes unity in the firm, the denominator, and the benchmark. All three fracture prior to theoretical closure.

## Figure 2. Scalar production of utilization

Shop floor → plant → corporate → financial governance → measurement apparatus

Caption:

Utilization is not chosen once; it is produced across organizational scales, each imposing distinct constraints, logics, and contradictions.

## Table 1. Literature clusters and what each destabilizes

| Literature cluster | What it destabilizes | Mechanism |
|---|---|---|
| Shop-floor control | Organizational unity | Discipline vs empowerment, routine politics |
| Operations | Tactical coherence | Bottlenecks, slack, product-mix complexity |
| Governance | Objective unity | Shareholder conflict, labor voice, financial horizons |
| Capacity accounting | Denominator unity | Competing definitions of capacity |
| Measurement | Scalar coherence | Aggregation above the firm, survey/statistical construction |
| Critical management | Ideological neutrality | Naturalization of contradiction as technique |
